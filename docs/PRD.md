# Product Requirements Document (PRD): AI-Driver Proficiency Platform

## 1. Executive Summary
By 2027, it is predicted that **75% of hiring processes** will require mandatory certifications for AI proficiency. This platform aims to move hiring from traditional credential-based models to **competency-based evaluations** that measure how a candidate applies AI within their specific workflow.

## 2. Problem Statement
Current hiring processes often lack a way to verify if a candidate can actually "drive" AI tools or if they simply rely on them blindly. There is a growing need for assessments that test **practical application** rather than theoretical knowledge.

## 3. Key Features & Requirements

### 3.1 Practical AI Task Sandbox
* **Requirement:** Create a simulated environment where candidates perform job-specific duties.
* **Functionality:** Candidates must demonstrate effective **prompt engineering** and the ability to use AI to **summarize and synthesize information** relevant to their role.

### 3.2 Output Verification Module
* **Requirement:** A testing mechanism to evaluate a candidate’s ability to audit AI content.
* **Functionality:** The system provides AI-generated results with intentional flaws. Candidates are scored on their ability to **verify accuracy and quality**, ensuring they can oversee the tool effectively.

### 3.3 Integrated Skill Evaluation ("Human-Plus")
* **Requirement:** Measure AI proficiency alongside essential human traits.
* **Functionality:** Assessments will evaluate how AI usage interacts with **critical thinking, creativity, and ethics**—skills that AI cannot easily replicate on its own.

### 3.4 Standardized Role-Based Certification
* **Requirement:** Generate industry-recognized credentials for CVs and LinkedIn.
* **Functionality:** Upon passing, the platform issues a **standardized certification** tailored to specific roles (e.g., Marketing, Legal, Engineering), meeting the rising demand for role-based AI requirements.

## 4. Target User Personas
* **Hiring Managers:** Looking for a reliable way to vet AI skills as part of a mandatory workflow.
* **Job Seekers:** Needing to prove their AI "driving" competency to stay competitive in a market where 75% of jobs will require it by 2027.

## 5. Success Metrics
* **Verification Accuracy:** The rate at which candidates successfully identify AI hallucinations in the Output Verification Module.
* **Certification Adoption:** The number of "AI-Driver" badges added to professional profiles (LinkedIn/CVs).
* **Workflow Efficiency:** Improvement in task completion time for candidates using AI tools in the Sandbox compared to manual benchmarks.
